
## Teaching at U Michigan
- <strong>ME 507 Atomistic Simulation of Materials</strong> (O(20) students, W26, F26)
    - Graduate level course on atomistic simulation for materials
    - Focus on AI/ML techniques, in particular ML interatomic potentials
    - We also cover sampling methods and outline emerging agentic methods
    - <a href="./assets/pdf/ME507_Syllabus.pdf" target="_new">Syllabus for Fall 26 term</a>

- <strong>ME 382 Mechanical Behavior of Materials</strong> (O(100) students, F25, W27)
    - Comprehensive overview of materials science topics for mechanical engineers 
    - Core course for all Mechanical Engineering majors
    - <a href="https://me.engin.umich.edu/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/ME-382-Course-Profile.pdf" target="_new">UM Course Profile</a>

- <strong>COMPLIT 200: Introduction to translation</strong> (O(30) students, F25, W26)
    - Four lecture mini-course on machine translation in the comparative literature department
    - History (Georgetown, Babelfish, n-grams) and present (transformers, mBERT)
    - Focus on demystification of LLM-based tools, and fundamental limitations (context, tails)
    - More public information will be available soon!
